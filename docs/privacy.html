Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026
Contact: support@smartwake.app

SmartWake ("we", "us") operates the SmartWake mobile application.

Information We Collect

Health & sensor data (on-device): With your permission, SmartWake reads heart rate, sleep, and motion data from Apple Health or Health Connect to estimate sleep stages. This data is processed primarily on your device.

Account data (optional): If you create an account, we store your email and profile preferences.

Purchase data: Subscription status is processed by Apple or Google. We do not store payment card numbers.

Usage data: Anonymous crash reports and analytics may be collected to improve the app.

How We Use Data

  • Provide Smart Wake alarm functionality
  • Display sleep estimates and analytics
  • Process subscriptions and cloud sync (Premium)
  • Improve app reliability

Medical Disclaimer

SmartWake provides sleep stage estimates only. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose or treat sleep disorders.

Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device with encryption. Premium cloud sync stores encrypted backups on our servers when enabled.

Third Parties

  • Apple (App Store, HealthKit, in-app purchases)
  • Google (Play Store, Health Connect, in-app purchases)

Your Rights

You can delete local data by uninstalling the app. Contact support@smartwake.app for account deletion requests.

Children's Privacy

SmartWake is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
