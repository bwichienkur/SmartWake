## Description

SmartWake is the intelligent alarm clock that helps you wake up at the right moment — during light sleep, within your chosen window — so you start the day feeling refreshed instead of groggy.

**SMART WAKE**
Set your earliest and latest wake times. SmartWake monitors estimated sleep stages using Apple Health, your Apple Watch, or phone sensors. When light sleep is detected, your alarm triggers. If not, you'll wake at your latest time — never oversleeping.

**WAKE-UP CHALLENGES**
Prove you're awake with math problems, shake challenges, barcode scans, memory games, puzzles, and more. Choose one challenge, a sequence, or random mode.

**SLEEP TRACKING**
View sleep duration, estimated deep/light/REM/awake stages, sleep score, efficiency, and an interactive timeline. Weekly charts help you spot trends.

**IMPORTANT:** Sleep stages shown are estimates based on sensor data and are not medical-grade. SmartWake is not a medical device.
